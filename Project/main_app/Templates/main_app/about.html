    About The Project


    In the path of programming and problem solving, mentors play a huge role for their mentees. In the beginning, a new programmer wouldn’t always know what to do or how to plan their learning paths. That is where mentors come as saviors. But it isn’t always easy to find a good and experienced mentor. Depending on the mentor, a programmer may become very good and go far or they might give up midways being demotivated and depressed. That is why we decided to build a system that would act as a mentor for programmers and will always be available to everybody. As such lowering the dependency on mentors for beginners.
